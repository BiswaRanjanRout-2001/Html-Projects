Delicious Pasta Recipe

A simple and testy italian dish you can cook at home

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt and paper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated permesan cheese
  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saute garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt and paper. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix in the boiled pasta and stir it well.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and parmesan before serving.
